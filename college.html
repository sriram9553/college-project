    B-Tech

    Sri Mittapalli College of Engineering is situated in Guntur in Andhra Pradesh state of India. Established in 2006, it is accredited from NAAC, ISO, AICTE and it is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada. SMCE, Guntur offers 16 courses across 2 streams namely Engineering, Management and across 3 degrees like B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA. SMCE campus is spread over 15 Acres. Hostel facility is available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Auditorium, Canteen, Computer Lab, Girls Hostel, Gym, Library, Medical Facilities, Cls. Room, Placement, Sports
